# SMS Follow-Up Templates — Dental Practice Audit
## CallCatcher Ops | callcatcherops.com

All SMS messages sent under TCPA compliance. Recipients must have opted in or provided prior express consent. Include opt-out language per CTIA guidelines.

---

## Template 1 — Post-Audit Delivery Follow-Up

**Use when:** Audit report has been sent and you're following up 24–48 hours later.

**Message:**

Hi {{name}}, this is [Your Name] from CallCatcher Ops. Just sent over the missed-call report for {{company}}. Any questions or want to walk through it? Reply here or call {{phone}}. Reply STOP to opt out.

---

## Template 2 — Post-Voicemail Follow-Up

**Use when:** You called their office and it went to voicemail; no callback received.

**Message:**

Hi {{name}} — tried calling {{company}} at {{phone}} and left a voicemail. This is [Your Name] from CallCatcher Ops. We offer a free missed-call audit for dental offices. Interested? Reply YES or STOP to opt out.

---

## Template 3 — Interest Confirmation

**Use when:** Contact replied "yes" or expressed interest in the audit via any channel.

**Message:**

Got it, {{name}} — we'll run the free missed-call report for {{company}}. Takes 48 hrs. We'll send it to this number or email. Can you confirm your email? — CallCatcher Ops | callcatcherops.com. Reply STOP to opt out.
