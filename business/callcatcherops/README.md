# CallCatcher Ops

**Positioning**
Missed call recovery and appointment booking for local service businesses.

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**
- Local service businesses with inbound calls: med spas, dental clinics, home services, clinics, franchised operators.
- 5–30 inbound calls/day.
- Missed calls after-hours or during peak times.
- Wants appointment bookings and visibility on call conversions.

**Primary Offer**
Recover missed calls and book appointments within 7 days via SMS call-back, routing, and booking automation.

**Secondary Offer**
AI Crawl Monetization package to convert existing website traffic into more qualified calls/forms.

**Proof/Advantage (Current)**
- Domain and email live: callcatcherops.com and hello@callcatcherops.com.
- Fast setup workflow and repeatable automation templates.
- Dedicated outreach engine scaffold in `autonomy/`.

**Core Outcomes**
- Fewer lost leads.
- Faster response time.
- Clear attribution of booked revenue from calls.
