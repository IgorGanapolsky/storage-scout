# AI Crawl Monetization Package

**Positioning**
Turn existing website traffic into AI-visible, conversion-ready demand.

**Best Fit**
- Service businesses and operators already getting inbound site traffic.
- Teams that want more qualified calls/forms without increasing ad spend first.
- Owners who need implementation plus measurable reporting.

**Tier 1: Crawl Baseline Sprint — $500 (one-time)**
- AI crawl/readability audit (indexing, structure, metadata, internal links).
- "Money page" map: which pages should convert and why.
- 14-day fix plan prioritized by expected revenue impact.
- Delivery: 2 business days.

**Tier 2: Crawl Monetization Build — $1,800 (one-time)**
- Everything in Tier 1.
- Implementation of priority technical/content fixes.
- Conversion upgrades on top pages (CTA blocks, contact/booking friction fixes).
- Analytics wiring for crawl-origin traffic to calls/forms.
- Delivery: 7 business days.

**Tier 3: Crawl Revenue Ops — $650/mo**
- Everything needed to maintain and compound results post-build.
- Monthly AI crawl health checks and issue remediation.
- Monthly content/structure experiments on top opportunity pages.
- KPI report: crawl visibility, qualified leads, and conversion rate trend.

**Optional Add-ons**
- Additional location/service cluster: $400 each.
- Legacy CMS cleanup sprint: $600.
- Monthly executive walkthrough call + action log: $200/mo.

**Primary CTA**
- Start with Tier 1 if no crawl baseline exists.
- Skip to Tier 2 when baseline findings are already known and access is ready.
