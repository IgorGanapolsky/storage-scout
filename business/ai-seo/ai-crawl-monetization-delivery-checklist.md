# AI Crawl Monetization Delivery Checklist

## 1. Kickoff (Day 0-1)
- [ ] Confirm offer tier sold (Tier 1, 2, or 3).
- [ ] Collect access: CMS, analytics, GSC, booking/form destination.
- [ ] Confirm primary revenue actions to optimize (call, form, booking).
- [ ] Freeze success metrics and reporting cadence.

## 2. Crawl Baseline (Day 1-2)
- [ ] Capture baseline crawl/index visibility state.
- [ ] Identify top conversion pages and top leakage pages.
- [ ] Prioritize fixes by impact vs. implementation effort.
- [ ] Deliver baseline summary + implementation plan.

## 3. Monetization Build (Day 2-7, Tier 2+)
- [ ] Apply technical crawlability fixes (indexing, metadata, linking, page structure).
- [ ] Upgrade conversion path on priority pages (clear CTA, reduced friction).
- [ ] Align page copy to buyer intent and service outcomes.
- [ ] Ensure site supports AI discovery assets where applicable (`llms.txt`, sitemap, robots, schema).

## 4. Tracking + QA (Day 6-7, Tier 2+)
- [ ] Wire conversion tracking for calls/forms/bookings.
- [ ] Validate event firing and source tagging in analytics.
- [ ] Run pre/post checks for indexing, page health, and conversion UX.
- [ ] Publish launch notes with completed changes.

## 5. Handoff (Day 7, Tier 1/2)
- [ ] Deliver final change log and remaining backlog.
- [ ] Deliver KPI baseline vs. post-launch snapshot.
- [ ] Confirm owner and due date for unresolved items.
- [ ] Confirm next-step decision: monthly ops retained or closed.

## 6. Monthly Ops Cadence (Tier 3)
- [ ] Monthly crawl health review and remediation.
- [ ] Monthly conversion test plan on highest-opportunity pages.
- [ ] Monthly KPI report with actions taken and next priorities.
- [ ] Monthly client review with decision log.
