# Weekly Sales Insights (2026-03-03)

Source: SaaS Growth Dispatch weekly insights PDF (2026-02-25 to 2026-03-03).

## What To Sell
- Sell outcomes, not feature lists.
- Speak to buyers/approvers, not just end users.
- Use outbound as primary demand generation channel.
- Use workshop-first offers for higher conversion.

## Messaging Rules
- Lead with business result: qualified pipeline and booked revenue.
- Avoid technical-first intros.
- Use tight positioning for one buyer persona per sequence.

## GTM Actions
1. Replace generic landing copy with buyer-outcome copy.
2. Run outbound sequence to one niche at a time.
3. Offer paid workshop/diagnostic as first conversion step.
4. Track revenue validation, not vanity engagement metrics.
