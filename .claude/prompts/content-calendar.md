# Content Calendar

Generate a monthly content calendar for CallCatcher Ops marketing.

## Prompt

```
Create a 30-day content calendar for CallCatcher Ops targeting {industry} business owners.

Channels: {channels}
Month: {month} {year}
Business goal this month: {goal}
Proof points available: {proof_points}

Content mix (per week):
- 2 educational posts (missed-call stats, industry benchmarks, "did you know" facts)
- 1 proof/case study post (audit results, before/after numbers, client wins)
- 1 CTA post (free baseline offer, booking link, demo invite)
- 1 engagement post (poll, question, hot take relevant to their industry)

Requirements:
- Every post under 150 words (social) or 300 words (email/blog)
- Include specific numbers where possible (not "many businesses" but "42% of dental practices")
- Tag posts as: [EDU], [PROOF], [CTA], or [ENGAGE]
- Suggest best posting time for {industry} audience
- Mark which days are sales-focused vs awareness-focused

Format as a table:
| Day | Channel | Type | Topic | Key Stat/Hook | CTA |
```

## Prompt — Email Subject Lines

```
Generate 10 email subject lines for a {campaign_type} campaign targeting {industry} owners.

Requirements:
- Under 50 characters each
- Include a number in at least 6 of 10
- No spam trigger words (free, guarantee, act now, limited time)
- Mix approaches: curiosity, pain point, social proof, direct benefit
- Personalization token {company} in at least 3

Format:
1. [Subject line] — [Approach used]
```
